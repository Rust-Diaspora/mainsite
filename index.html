Welcome to Rust Diaspora

Rust Diaspora is a vanilla Rust server, no decay. With monthly map wipe (first thursday of every month), and only forced blueprints wipe only.

We aim to create a friendly place for people to build, farm, enjoy and make friends.

HAPPY RUSTING, but dont forget to read server rules


Rules

Valve Anti-Cheat (VAC) Bans & Game Bans

  • You are allowed one (1) VAC or game ban across all accounts if it is over 180 days.
  • You are allowed two (2) VAC or game bans across all accounts if the most recent is over 540 days.
  • More than 2 VAC or game bans are not allowed.
  • Groups caught harboring hackers or ban evaders will be banned in whole. Do NOT play with known cheaters.
  • There are absolutely NO exceptions to this rule. The answer will always be no so do not ask.

Racism

  • We have absolutely zero tolerance for any form of racism or bigotry. You can do whatever you like in your day to day lives, but it will not be tolerated on our servers.
  • Do not type it, do not write or draw it on a sign, and do not say in in VoIP chat.

Disrespecting Staff & Players

  • Just because you are hiding behind your monitor does not mean you get to leave your morals at the login screen. Don't be a piece of garbage and try to be a decent human being.
  • If your friend broke the rules and was punished for it, don't try to argue with an admin about our decision.

Gameplay

  • Max group size is 4.
  • No base overtaking.
    - Base blocking during raid is allowed
    - Raider must gives back access to owner after the raid (removes door, write code in a note..)
  • No MLRS raids (because of MLRS may causes foundation wipes on small bases)
